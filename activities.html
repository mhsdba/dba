Activities

Team building Motivational Activities

In addition to providing an academic environment, the Digital Business Academy also considers the interests and enjoyment of its students. Outside of the parameters we establish for them in the classroom, every student in the DBA deserves the highest quality of environment. Through events like the Town Hall Meeting/Mixer, Academy, Olympics, Wacky Day/Awards, and End of Year Academy (Academic) Awards, our kids get to experience the rewards of their hard work.

Face-to-Face Career Mentor Program

Some outstanding mentors with extensive expertise in the job fields our students aspire to follow beyond college will be introduced to our students. In order to succeed in today's environment, they assist pupils in developing practical objectives and abilities. For a head start in the real world, the DBA offers all of our juniors a mentor match, monthly mentor meetings, and a mentor end-of-year celebration.

Job Shadow Opportunities

We collaborate with NAF academies each year to offer our high school students some employment chances so they can be ready for their professional lives both during and after college. We assist them with all the tools, and interview preparation such as mock interviews through the City of Milpitas, resume writing, and other particulars related to securing an internship. The following firms provide us with internship opportunities: NextFlex, Google, Facebook, AT&T, Verizon, and Cisco.

Curriculum Enrichment Tours

Students are given the opportunity to go on a variety of field trips each year that complement the curriculum. DBA provides curriculum enrichment tours to locations such as Angel Island with an Integrated curriculum unit on Immigration, San Jose Tech Museum or Computer History Museum, DeYoung Museum or Oakland Museum, Museum of Modern Art MOMA, Academy of Science Museum, Computer History Museum, to learn more about a topic particularly important in the time period.

College Tours

Not only do we encourage students to attend curricular enrichment tours, but we also provide college tours in the area so that students can choose the college they want to go to or at least get a sense of what college life entails. We take our students to CSU East Bay/Ohlone College, UC Santa Cruz, San Francisco State University, Academy of Art University, Santa Clara University/Foothill College, and San Jose State University/De Anza College. A college tour to multiple institutions and colleges complements the college application process and is highly persuasive when it comes to the college essay that applicants must write to be admitted.
