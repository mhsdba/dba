About DBA

What is DBA?

The Digital Business Academy at Milpitas High School prepares students for college and career. High-quality, hands-on Career Technical Education instructors provide students with classroom and industry experiences. The Digital Business Academy is grant-funded through the California Department of Education and follows the requirements of the State CPA Academy Program Model (in addition to the regular high school curriculum).

Guest Speakers

The Digital Business Academy invites industry specialists as a guest speaker for the academy students to get an overview of the real world.
